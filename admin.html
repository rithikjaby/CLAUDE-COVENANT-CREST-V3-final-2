Netlify Email Notifications Setup

To receive email alerts when someone submits a form on your website, complete these steps:

  1. Log in to app.netlify.com and go to your site
  2. Click Forms in the left sidebar
  3. For each of the three forms (contact, haulage-quote, trade-enquiry): click the form name → Form notifications → Add notification → Email → enter jaby.k@covenantcrest.co.uk
  4. Save. You will now receive an email for every new submission.
